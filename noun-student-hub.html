Registration & Portal Guide

Navigating the NOUN student portal is the first step to a successful semester. Our guides simplify the process for both new and returning students.

How to Register & Navigate the NOUN Portal

The official portal is the central hub for all academic activities, from signing up to checking your progress. You can access it directly at nouonline.nou.edu.ng.

  • For New Students: Your first step is to sign up using the 'Fresh student' icon. Be mindful of your password and security question.[6]
  • For Registered Students: You can log in with your matriculation number and password via the 'Registered student' icon.[6]

The portal itself has on-screen guides and directives that can assist you in your registration process, including course and exam registration. There are a variety of essential activities you can perform on the portal, from printing your student ID card to dropping courses for an exam.

Important Tip for Payments: During payment on the third-party site, Remita, it is strongly advised that you do not interact with the keyboard, mouse, or screen until you are automatically redirected back to the NOUN page to avoid any payment interruptions.

TMA Guidance & E-Exam Preparation

Tutor-Marked Assignments (TMAs) and E-Exams are a core part of your academic life at NOUN. We provide resources to help you meet deadlines and maximize your scores.

How to Complete Your TMA

For your TMAs, the NOUN e-learning portal is your go-to destination. Here, you will find courses, resources, and a system to complete and submit your assignments. Access the e-learning portal directly at https://elearn.nou.edu.ng/.

  • **Current Procedures:** After logging in with your matriculation number and password, you can navigate to the relevant course to find and complete your TMA. There are also video tutorials available to help you with the process.
  • **Mobile-Friendly:** You can conveniently complete your TMAs using your mobile phone, allowing you to study and submit assignments from anywhere.

GST302 Business Plan Topics

The GST302 business plan is a key course requirement that helps you develop a business idea. We provide technological topics that align with the course's thematic areas and a detailed format to ensure your plan is investor-ready.

Thematic Areas & Sample Topics

Your business plan should focus on one of the four thematic areas: Agripreneur, Biotechnology, Green Education, or **Information Communication Technology (ICT)**.[12] Here are some ICT-focused topics you can explore:

  • Software Development & Web Designing
  • Content Automation
  • Repairs of Electrical Appliances
  • 21st Century 3-D Business Centre
  • Online Gaming Centre
